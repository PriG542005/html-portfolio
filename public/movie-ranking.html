My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Social Network

The movie serves to represent the journey Mark Zuckerberg(Jesse Eisenberg)undertook in relation to the formation of Facebook.

The Imitation Game

It is based on the real life story of legendary cryptanalyst Alan Turing.

The Social Dilemma
